Common Questions

What are the entry requirements?

expand_more

Most of our short courses only require basic literacy and physical fitness to handle workshop tools. However, advanced programs like Coded Welding require prior NQF levels or a passing grade in our Trade Assessment.

Are the courses practical or theoretical?

expand_more

Our training is 100% focused on hands-on capability. While there is necessary safety and theory covered in the classroom, the vast majority of your time is spent in the workshop or on active building sites.

Do you offer payment plans?

expand_more

Yes, we understand that funding education can be difficult. Contact our administration office to discuss flexible payment schedules for our longer Masterclass and Certification courses.
