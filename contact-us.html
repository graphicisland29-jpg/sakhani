Frequently Asked Questions

Are your courses SETA and QCTO accredited? expand_more

Yes, Sakheni Ma Africa is a fully accredited QCTO training provider (SDP160625231032). All our core technical courses are aligned with major South African SETAs, ensuring your certification carries weight in the job market.

Do I need previous experience to enroll? expand_more

No prior experience is strictly required for our foundational courses! We bridge the artisan gap by taking you from absolute basics to advanced, industry-ready practical mastery.

How long do the practical training courses take? expand_more

Course durations vary depending on the specific trade (Welding, Plumbing, or Bricklaying) and the level of certification you are pursuing. Contact us directly or use the form above to get a customized timeline and fee structure for your chosen path.
