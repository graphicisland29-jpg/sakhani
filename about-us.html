A Legacy of Practical Skill Development

Sakheni Ma Africa stands as a specialized training institution focused on empowering South Africans with tangible, marketable industrial skills. We understand that the backbone of a growing economy lies in its artisan workforce.

Located in Nigel, Gauteng, we provide a focused environment where students move beyond theory. Our training methodology emphasizes hands-on mastery in core trades such as welding, plumbing, and bricklaying, ensuring every graduate is ready for the rigors of the modern construction and engineering sectors.

Our Obligation

Empowering Southern Africa

Sakheni Portable Skills and Development recognizes our obligation to raise the profile of education, training, and skills development within Southern Africa.

Our institution is built by individuals endowed with a wealth of skills, knowledge, and competencies gained through extensive learning and practical industry exposure.

Through our strategically specialized structure, we deliver excellent, affordable training initiatives. We ensure quality learning for previously disadvantaged individuals, equipping them with capabilities that directly benefit the broader economy.
